# OpsZ Product Roadmap

This roadmap outlines our planned capabilities for 2026. Timelines and features may change based on customer feedback and market needs.

---

## Q1 2026 (Current Quarter)

### Infrastructure & Reliability
- **Multi-cloud inventory expansion** — GCP and Azure metadata schemas for unified cloud discovery
- **Stream pipeline hardening** — Enhanced reliability and error handling for event streaming
- **End-to-end regression suite** — Comprehensive automated testing for platform stability

### Job Execution
- **Job processor enhancement** — Direct Kafka stream consumption for agent dispatch
- **Workflow execution improvements** — Better handling of complex DAGs and long-running jobs
- **Status tracking refinements** — Improved real-time log and status collection

### Integrations
- **ServiceNow** — Bi-directional incident and change management
- **PagerDuty** — Alert-triggered workflow execution
- **Slack** — Notifications and interactive approvals
- **Jira** — Issue-driven workflow triggers

### Platform
- **Deployment packaging improvements** — Streamlined installation experience
- **Monitoring and observability** — Enhanced platform metrics and health checks
